Mandi

Mandi is a traditional dish of meat and rice that originated in Yemen's Hadhramaut region. It's a staple dish in many parts of the Arabian Peninsula and is also found in Egypt, Turkey, the Levant, and Southeast Asia.

Biriyani

Biryani is typically made with rice, meat or seafood, and spices, but can also include vegetables, eggs, or potatoes. The meat can be chicken, goat, lamb, beef, prawn, or fish.

Friedrice

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that is stir-fried in a wok or frying pan and typically includes other ingredients like eggs, vegetables, meat, or seafood. It can be eaten on its own or as a side dish
